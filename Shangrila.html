Shangrila

Lower Kachura Lake, better known as Shangrila Lake is located in the Kachura Village, Skardu, Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. The lake has a height of 2,500 meters and became Shangrila Lake, when in 1983, Shangrila Resort was built at the bank of this lake. Shangrila Resort was the first resort opened in Skardu, Gilgit-Baltistan. It was the result of sheer hard work of Brigadier Muhammad Aslam Khan Afridi, who founded this beautiful resort at the bank of astonishing lake. One of the reasons for the fame of this lake is its heart-like shape. Tourists love to watch the shape of this lake and enjoy the scenic moments.

It is one of the most visited and most loved lakes in Pakistan. It is a peaceful place far from the hustle-bustle of the city. One can refresh himself by paying a visit to the Shangrila Resort and enjoy some time watching the beauty of nature. You will forget the problems of the outer world and it will give you a sense of pleasure. Tourists visiting the lovely Shangrila Lake can enjoy the view of the magnificent lake from their hotels. They can enjoy a good dinner with their loved ones in the restaurant. One can relish himself by boating, filming, and can make profound memories while taking photographs.

Shangrila Resort is the best place for the newlywed couples to enjoy their honeymoon. They will get a chance to understand each other even better and while going back, they will have several memories for life.
