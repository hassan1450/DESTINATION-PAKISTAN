DESTINATION PAKISTAN

OVERVEW:

Pakistan, often misunderstood, is a land of vibrant culture,
tradition, and natural beauty, perfect for adventurous travelers.
This comprehensive tour of Northern Pakistan offers a deep dive
into its stunning landscapes and rich heritage.Starting in Islamabad,
you journey through the Swat Valley, stay with the Kalash tribe, and
traverse the Shandur Pass to reach Gilgit. Explore the beautiful Hunza,
descend the Karakoram Highway, and visit Shigar, Khaplu, and Skardu.
The tour includes the Deosai Plateau, Fairy Meadows, and ends back in Islamabad
via Naran. With over two decades of experience, we provide authentic and
innovative trips with expert guides, transport, and accommodation.
